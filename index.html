FAQ

How many team members can I invite?

You can invite as many team members as you'd like! We encourage collaboration and growth within our platform, so feel free to expand your team as needed.

What is the maximum file upload size

No more than 2GB. All files in your account must fit your alloted storage size

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, visit your account settings and click on the "Forgot Password" link. Follow the instructions sent to your email to securely reset it

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you have the flexibility to cancel your subscription at any time. Just go to the "Billing" or "Subscription" section in your account settings and follow the cancellation process.

Do you provide additional support?

Absolutely! We offer various support options, including email, chat, and phone support, depending on your subscription plan.
